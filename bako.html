Bako| About


Bako is the clothing and shoe line division of Ballers Africa. We provide apparel and clothing-related services for Ballers Africa and our customers, with a focus on high-quality products and excellent customer service. We offer a wide range of clothing items and accessories, from t-shirts and caps to sneakers and boots, and so much more for both men and women.

Our Services

  • Clothing lines for men and women
  • Shoe lines for men and women
  • Custom apparel design
  • Apparel printing and embroidery
  • Clothing and shoe repairs and alterations
  • Online shopping and delivery
