The BA Arena


 

Games

Ba. Arena offers a wide range of interactive video games, 9D graphic experiences, and other recreational games for individuals, groups, and families.

3X3 Basketball

Ba. Arena features 3x3 basketball courts and support facilities for active sport, including continental league games, recreation games, and training sessions. We also offer mentorship and coaching programs for aspiring athletes.

Food Courts

Ba. Arena has a variety of food courts to cater to our customers' tastes and preferences. Our food courts feature a range of cuisines and beverages to satisfy all cravings. We also have television lounges for customers to enjoy their meals while watching live sports events.

Healthcare

Ba. Arena has a branch of Ba. Health to provide minor health services for athletes and customers. Our healthcare services ensure the well-being of our customers and athletes during their time at the arena.

Beauty Services

Ba. Arena has branches of Fancie Jewels and Bako to handle apparel, beauty, hair, makeup, and jewelry needs for both ladies and gentlemen. We offer a range of beauty services to enhance the overall experience of our customers at the arena.
