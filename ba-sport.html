BA Sport


 

BA Sport is a subsidiary of Ballers Africa that focuses on promoting and developing various sports across the continent, beyond the 3X3 basketball format that BA Arena specializes in. Our aim is to provide world-class training, mentorship, and resources to aspiring athletes and coaches, as well as organize and support various sports events and leagues that showcase African talent and potential. Our offerings include:

  • Training programs and camps for various sports.
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities with experienced coaches, athletes, and industry professionals
  • Access to state-of-the-art facilities, equipment, and resources that enable optimal performance and development
  • Participation in local and international sports events, competitions, and leagues that provide exposure and recognition

At BA Sport, we believe that sports have the power to transform lives, communities, and nations, and we are committed to creating opportunities and platforms that enable African athletes to reach their full potential and impact the world positively.
